BULLET PORTRAIT

During my studies, I created an illustrated book for the wrongfully imprisoned called "The Face of InJustice".

This is one of my case studies from the book, Huujilt. He was wrongfully convicted for a murder in China and was sharply killed by firing squad before anyone knew he was innocent.

In order to creatively exhibit this man's story, I made a portrait that is made up of thousands of bullets.

Produced with Maxon Cinema 4D Software.

MOTION GRAPHICS
COMPETITION BRIEF - ADVERTISING CONCEPT VIDEO

As a part of my studies, we were asked to fulfill a design competition brief, my chosen brief was for Netflix.

The aim was to create a marketing campaign to promote foreign content to a UK audience. For this I had done research into quiz culture and identified a way for Netflix to incorporate a quiz feature into their UI. Viewers would be able to swipe left/right to answer their preferences and be given recommended film and tv shows to watch. I called this GLOBALFLIX.

Produced with Maxon Cinema 4D, Adobe After Effects, Adobe XD, and Adobe Illustrator.

PRINT MEDIA
Screen Printing

As a part of my studies, I have learned how to create design/artwork using multiple techniques, such as screen printing and lino printing.

This piece was born from a group project for which we were given the theme of 'Jungle'. Me and the 3 others began to brainstorm, where the idea of the lyrics "My head is a jungle" was chosen. From this we were required to create individual designs supporting our collective idea. This way, we all had a unique spin on the design. These were then taken to lino, where we all produced our own lino cutting. The prints were then used to create a digital file specifying a 2 colour separation.

Out of the 4 outcomes from the group, my design was chosen to be our collective group design.

This was then used to create screen prints using 2 colours; pink and green.
